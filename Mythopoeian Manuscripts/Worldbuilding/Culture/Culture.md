### [[Four Divine Forces]]
is not correlated with the greek element model but has several similarities in structure, connection to the four winds, and four elements
- Magical Objects Spell: These spells are properties that can be attached to any object that is not living. These properties can be stacked and changed to create things with ever more complexity. They instruct how a thing behaves and interacts with an object's original properties. The effects of a spell are often labeled with runes. This allows others to know what properties have been changed, how an object's original properties are affected and allows spell casters to more accurately keep track of their spell work. A spell that runs out of energy will stop its affects and the object with revert back to its original properties 
- Life Magic (Magical Biology Spell): MB spells manipulate the magical properties of a living thing. Nothing of the physical being is changed, just the magical properties. However, one must be very careful how to incorporate the MB spell, because it might interfere with the natural biology of the organism. For example, if you wanted your hamster to float, you need to make sure that every part of the hamster will be lifted, lest some of its organs be displaced. The darker side to this is that a living thing can be made to carry out certain tasks, as long as they have a source of magic to power the spell. Strict laws have been put in place to limit this.
- Physical Objects Spell: These spells alter the physical properties of an object directly, changing the object in some way, usually in a way that is not easily reversible. They are much more energy intensive. It is often used to regulate chemical reactions and shape materials, both for industry and personal machining.
- Physical Biology Spell: The PB spell is used to manipulate the physical shape of a living organism. The state of its magical properties are not affected, just its shape. However, it cannot affect any living thing that has a soul in it, or is protected by soul energy. This type of spell has great potential for good, and an even greater potential for evil. Obviously it can be used for medical reasons, healing, and the like, but it can also be used for biological experimentation.

A local space for recycling or reusing plastic containers, glass jars, and any other reusable household items, has a general steampunk/solar punk theme, and would be most effective in remote towns like on mountain sides or in desert valleys, it has several larger locations within cities that recycle plastics, glass, rubber and metals. The place was originally a small shack in a smaller town disconnected from trade routes, the shack allowed people to Signage the town's supply of containers, and it was mostly known for its safe storage of mason jars, and thus the name was born. After the town was abandoned, one of the people who maintained the shack decided to make a business out of it and bought a small building to sell reused household items the most popular of which was kitchen containers
