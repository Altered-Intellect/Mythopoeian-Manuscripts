# Welcome to the ✨️Mythopoeian Manuscripts✨️
This is a worldbuilding project that has been rattling around in my brain for the better part of a decade. It is love letter to fantasy, sci-fi, and myths. It is in equal parts fantastic and realistic, creating detailed and interconnected lore for the many tropes and stereotypes in cultural storytelling in the best way I know how, worldbuilding.

A work in progress that has taken many forms but has ultimately consumed so many ideas and stories that I thought should finnally be published in some capacity. This is also an attempt to get myself to actively cultivate content to communicate to others as most of my writing and worldbuilding has been for the benefit of myself and headspace.

I will not promise consistent updates but I encourage questions! 

My only prerequisite for fanart and derivative work is that you credit and send it to me to look at. (please include content warnings, of course) I'd love to see how my stuff resonates with others. 

Thank you for taking the time for giving my stuff a try. Now lets explore the Myths together!
